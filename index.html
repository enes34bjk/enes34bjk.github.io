Online quiz application for visually impaired people

Helping to Enhance Knowledge

Our project is aimed towards visually impaired people. We are hoping to increase the quality of their educational life. They can solve questions and test their knowledge. They can upload their question, create their quiz and share it with other users. Users can upload questions by typing or they can simply upload an image which contains the text of the question, our program will extract it the text from the image. Additionally voice questions can be uploaded, user can record a speech and upload it as question. Users will listen the question via text to speech feature and can answer the question with the help of talkback accessibility feature. If they like users can create custom quiz which will be accessible only by their account and can be modified.
