Cs491 senior design project

Helping to Enhance Knowledge

Our project is aimed towards visually impaired people. We are hoping to increase the quality of their educational life. They can solve quizzes and test their knowledge. They can upload their question, create their quiz and share it with other users. Since our application uses accessibility features, such as text to speech and voice control, users can use our application with minimum aid. Users don't have to type down their questions, they can upload their questions as text image, our application will convert it automatically.
